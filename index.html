Linux Torvalds

The Creator of Linux

Biography

Linux Torvalds is a finnish american software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of the Linux kernal , Which is the kernal for a number of operating systems > he also created the version control system git . Torvalds was born on december 28, 1969 in helsinki , finland . He studied Computer science at the university of Helensinki and developed a linux kernal as a student project in 1991 . Since then , the lInux kernal has become one of the most widely used kernal in the world , powering everything from smartphones to supercomputers

Achievements

  • 1991 - Created kernal and the version control system git
  • 2004 - Named one of the "100 Most Influential People in the World" by Time magazine in2004
  • 2010 - Named one of the "100 Most Influential People in the World" by Time magazine in2004
  • 2010 - Received an honorary doctorate from the University of Helsinki in 2010
  • 2013 - Inducted into the Internet Hall of Fame in 2013 for his contributions to the development of the Linux kernel and the creation of Git
  • 2014- Inducted into the Hall of Fame of the Computer History Museum in 2014
  • 2015- Received the 2015 IEEE Computer Society Computer Pioneer Award
  • 2019- Received the 2019 ACM SIGOPS Hall of Fame Award
  • 2021 - Awarded the Takeda Foundation's 2021 Takeda Award for Social Contribution, which recognizes individuals who have made significant contributions to society through technology
  • 2021 - Received the 2021 IEEE Masaru Ibuka Consumer Electronics Award, which recognizes individuals who have made significant contributions to the field of consumer electronics
